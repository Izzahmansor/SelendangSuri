About Selendang Seri

Welcome to Selendang Seri, where elegance meets empowerment. Founded in 2024 by three passionate entrepreneurs—Zara, Fatin, and Izzah—we are on a mission to redefine modest fashion by creating premium shawls that blend tradition, comfort, and modern style. Our journey began with a shared vision to celebrate the beauty, grace, and individuality of every Muslim woman.

At Selendang Seri, every shawl tells a story—one of strength, elegance, and cultural pride. Whether you’re dressing for everyday wear or a special occasion, our collection offers a seamless fusion of functionality and artistry.

Meet Our Founders

Zara: The creative heart of Selendang Seri, Zara’s designs are inspired by the harmony of Malaysian heritage and contemporary trends. Her passion for textiles brings each shawl to life.

Fatin: The strategist and marketing expert, Fatin connects our brand with women across Malaysia and beyond. Her vision fosters a global community of modest fashion lovers.

Izzah: The perfectionist behind the quality of every shawl, Izzah ensures that each piece embodies excellence, from sourcing the finest fabrics to crafting enduring designs.

Our Home

Selendang Seri is proudly based in the vibrant city of Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Inspired by its rich cultural tapestry and modern spirit, our headquarters is a creative hub where tradition meets innovation. From the lush beauty of Malaysia’s landscapes to its dynamic urban culture, every aspect of our surroundings influences our work.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower women with shawls that inspire confidence and reflect individuality while staying true to their values of modesty and elegance.

Vision: To become a global leader in modest fashion, celebrated for creativity, craftsmanship, and a commitment to empowering women worldwide.

Our Core Values

  • Quality: Ensuring each shawl offers unmatched comfort and durability.
  • Empowerment: Inspiring women to embrace their unique style with confidence.
  • Community: Building a supportive, inspiring space for modest fashion enthusiasts.
  • Innovation: Redefining modest fashion through creative, forward-thinking designs.
